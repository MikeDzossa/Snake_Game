----------------------------------------------------------
		     Instructions
----------------------------------------------------------

1. You have 3 lives. You lose one everytime you die.
2. You die when the snake bites the walls or his body.
3. The snake increases in size as he bites food.
4. Once all the lives are lost, it's "GAME OVER".
5. To pause the game, press any key different from the
   direction keys.

----------------------------------------------------------
		      Key Mapping
----------------------------------------------------------
       Left = "Left Arrow"    Right = "Right Arrow"
           Up = "Up Arrow"    Left = "Down Arrow"
